About

I'm a Flutter developer based in Giza, Egypt, focused on turning messy, paper-based, or legacy workflows into fast, reliable mobile apps.

At The Arab Contractors, I architect and maintain three cross-platform Flutter apps that replaced paper-based workflows across four departments — cutting manual processing time by roughly 60%. I work in 4–8 week agile release cycles, sitting with stakeholders to shape requirements rather than just implementing tickets handed down to me.

Before that, I contributed to four Flutter projects at Easy Pro Code, freelanced across fintech, e-commerce and travel apps, and interned at HDS Technology in Dubai. Across all of it, the constant is Clean Architecture and MVVM with Bloc/Cubit — I'd rather spend an extra day getting the architecture right than pay for it in bugs later.
